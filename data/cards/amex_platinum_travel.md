# American Express Platinum Travel Credit Card — Terms, Reward Rules & Transfer Partners

Issuer: American Express
Reward currency: Membership Rewards Points
Retrieved date: 2026-07-15
Source: compiled from public sources listed below — NOT official issuer documentation; verify current terms with the issuer.

## Reward Earning Structure
- Base rate: 1 Membership Rewards (MR) Point per ₹50 spent on eligible purchases (i.e. 2 MR per ₹100).
- No enhanced category multipliers; the base rate applies uniformly to all eligible spend.
- Note: Spends in the excluded categories below earn no base MR Points, but they still count toward the annual milestone spend thresholds (verify — widely reported by aggregators, confirm with issuer T&Cs).
- Points do not expire while the card is active and in good standing; points are forfeited within ~30 days of card cancellation.

## Exclusions (no Membership Rewards Points earned)
- Fuel
- Utilities
- Insurance
- Cash transactions / cash advances
- EMI conversion at point of sale
(Per the official product page wording: "except for spends on fuel, utilities, insurance, cash transactions and EMI conversion at point of sale.")

## Milestone Benefits
Current structure (restructured effective 9 March 2026), per Cardmembership year:
- Welcome gift (first year only): 10,000 MR Points for spending ₹15,000 within 90 days of Cardmembership (after paying the annual fee).
- Spend ₹1.9 Lakh in a membership year → 7,500 bonus MR Points.
- Spend ₹4 Lakh in a membership year → additional 10,000 bonus MR Points.
- Spend ₹7 Lakh in a membership year → additional 22,500 bonus MR Points + a Taj Stay voucher worth ₹10,000 (redeemable at Taj, SeleQtions and Vivanta hotels; once per Cardmembership year).
- Total bonus MR if all three tiers are hit: 40,000 MR Points + ₹10,000 Taj voucher (plus the 10,000 welcome points in year one).
- Note: the Taj voucher moved from the old ₹4 Lakh tier up to the new ₹7 Lakh tier (the widely reported 2026 devaluation).

## Reward Caps
- No specific monthly or annual cap on base earning has been identified in public sources (verify).
- Milestone bonuses and the Taj voucher are each awarded once per Cardmembership year.
- Program-wide transfer limit: a maximum of 900,000 MR Points can be transferred per calendar year (verify).

## Redemption Options
- Transfer to airline and hotel loyalty partners (best value; see below).
- "Platinum Travel Collection" / travel bookings and vouchers (e.g., Taj Experiences e-Gift Cards) via the Membership Rewards catalogue.
- Merchandise / brand vouchers (e.g., Flipkart) through the rewards catalogue.
- Statement credit ("Pay with Points") — lowest value.

## Point Valuation
- Statement credit: approximately ₹0.25 per MR Point (verify).
- Catalogue / voucher redemptions (e.g., Flipkart): roughly ₹0.30 per MR Point (verify).
- Taj voucher redemption via Platinum Travel Collection: roughly ₹0.50 per MR Point (verify).
- Travel-partner (airline/hotel) transfers: typically ₹0.50 to ₹1.50+, and higher for premium-cabin sweet spots (verify — depends on award chart and availability).

## Transfer Partners
Note: The Amex India program transfers airline points at a 2:1 ratio (2 MR = 1 partner mile), unlike the US 1:1 program. The exact partner list, ratios, and minimums are inconsistently reported across aggregators and change frequently — treat all entries below as (verify) against amex.com/in. Amex India offers roughly 8 airline and 2 hotel partners.

- Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer (airline) — transfer ratio 2:1. Minimum transfer ~800 MR points (verify).
- British Airways Executive Club / Avios (airline) — transfer ratio 2:1. Minimum transfer ~1,200 MR points (verify).
- Emirates Skywards (airline) — transfer ratio 2:1. Minimum transfer ~800 MR points (verify).
- Etihad Guest (airline) — transfer ratio 2:1. Minimum transfer ~500 MR points (verify).
- Air France–KLM Flying Blue (airline) — transfer ratio 2:1. Minimum transfer ~500 MR points (verify).
- Qatar Airways Privilege Club / Avios (airline) — transfer ratio 2:1. Minimum transfer ~500 MR points (verify).
- Cathay (Asia Miles) (airline) — transfer ratio 2:1. Minimum transfer ~800 MR points (verify).
- Virgin Atlantic Flying Club (airline) — transfer ratio 2:1. Minimum transfer ~800 MR points (verify).
- Marriott Bonvoy (hotel) — transfer ratio 1:1. Minimum transfer ~1,000 MR points (verify).
- Hilton Honors (hotel) — transfer ratio 1 MR : 0.90 Hilton points. Minimum transfer ~1,000 MR points (verify).

## Annual Fee & Waiver
- Joining/annual fee: ₹5,000 + GST (₹5,900 inclusive of 18% GST).
- Renewal fee: ₹5,000 + GST per year.
- No automatic fee waiver is published; the card is frequently offered "First Year Free" via referral/promotional links (verify). The card's value proposition effectively "pays back" the fee only for high spenders reaching the milestones.
- Note: As of early 2026, Amex reportedly paused new applications for this card in India (verify).
