# HDFC Diners Club Black Credit Card — Terms, Reward Rules & Transfer Partners

Issuer: HDFC Bank
Reward currency: Reward Points
Retrieved date: 2026-07-15
Source: compiled from public sources listed below — NOT official issuer documentation; verify current terms with the issuer.

## Reward Earning Structure
- Base earning: 5 Reward Points per ₹150 spent on retail (non-accelerated) transactions — approximately a 3.33% return when points are redeemed at ₹1 each.
- SmartBuy accelerated earning: up to 10X Reward Points (up to 50 RP per ₹150) on eligible spends routed through the HDFC SmartBuy portal.
  - 10X currently applies to Hotel bookings, IGP, Jockey and PharmEasy (categories/brands change at bank discretion — verify).
  - 5X applies to Flight bookings, Bus bookings, Myntra, Duty-Free (Adani One), MakeMyTrip holiday packages (verify).
  - 3X applies to Train bookings, Brand Vouchers, and Apple products (verify).
- Direct (non-SmartBuy) spends earn only the base 5 RP per ₹150. Accelerated (5X/10X) multipliers apply only when the transaction is booked through the SmartBuy platform for the eligible category/brand.

## Exclusions (no Reward Points earned)
The following categories typically earn no Reward Points (standard HDFC exclusions — verify against the current MITC):
- Rent payments
- Fuel
- Wallet loads / prepaid card loads
- EMI transactions
- Government / tax payments
- Education (via third-party apps, in some cases) (verify)

## Reward Caps
- Overall cap: maximum 75,000 Reward Points per statement cycle.
- SmartBuy accelerated (5X/10X) cap: maximum ~2,500 RP per day and ~7,500 RP per calendar month of accelerated (bonus) points via SmartBuy (verify — exact wording differs by source).
- Brand-voucher sub-cap: effective 1 July 2026, accelerated Reward Points on brand vouchers bought via SmartBuy are capped at approximately 3,000 RP per month per card (verify).

## Milestone Benefits
- Quarterly: 10,000 bonus Reward Points on spends of ₹4,00,000 in a calendar quarter (verify current threshold).
- Annual/renewal: renewal (annual) fee waived on meeting the annual spend condition (see Annual Fee & Waiver below).
- (Some legacy/metal variants also carried complimentary memberships e.g. Club Marriott, Forbes — verify current welcome/milestone bundle with the issuer.)

## Redemption Options
- SmartBuy flights & hotels: redeem at 1 Reward Point = ₹1. Points can cover up to 70% of the booking value, with the balance charged to the card.
- Transfer to airline/hotel partners (AirMiles): 1 RP = 1 partner point/mile for 1:1 partners; other partners at 2:1 (see Transfer Partners).
- Products / rewards catalogue: 1 RP = up to ₹0.50.
- Cashback / statement credit: 1 RP = ₹0.30.

## Point Valuation
- Assumed value for flights/hotels via SmartBuy and for 1:1 airmile transfers: ₹1.00 per Reward Point.
- Assumed value for catalogue redemption: ₹0.50 per Reward Point.
- Assumed value for cashback: ₹0.30 per Reward Point.

## Annual Fee & Waiver
- Annual/renewal fee: ₹10,000 + applicable taxes.
- Renewal fee waiver: waived on annual spends of ₹8,00,000 in the preceding 12 months for the current Metal Edition (verify). The older/legacy (non-metal) Diners Club Black historically offered the waiver at ₹5,00,000 annual spend (verify).
