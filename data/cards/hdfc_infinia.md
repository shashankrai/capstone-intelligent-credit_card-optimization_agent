# HDFC Infinia Credit Card — Terms, Reward Rules & Transfer Partners

Issuer: HDFC Bank
Reward currency: Reward Points
Retrieved date: 2026-07-15
Source: compiled from public sources listed below — NOT official issuer documentation; verify current terms with the issuer.

## Reward Earning Structure
- Base earning: 5 Reward Points per ₹150 spent on retail (effective ~3.3% at ₹1/point). This applies to most standard categories.
- SmartBuy accelerated earning (HDFC Bank's SmartBuy portal only, not direct merchant spends):
  - Hotels: up to 10X (effective ~33.3%) (verify — current portal wording sometimes describes this as 5X)
  - Flights: 5X
  - Trains / rail bookings: 3X (verify)
  - SmartBuy brand gift vouchers (Gyftr / Woohoo): 5X
- The accelerated multiplier is the base 5X plus bonus points; it applies only to bookings routed through SmartBuy. Direct spends outside SmartBuy earn only the base 5 RP / ₹150.

## Exclusions (no Reward Points earned)
Categories reported to earn zero Reward Points (verify each against the current MITC, as HDFC has repeatedly revised these):
- Rent payments (e.g., via CRED, NoBroker, etc.)
- Fuel
- Wallet loads
- Government / tax payments — Income Tax, GST
- EMI conversions
- Insurance premiums (verify — historically Infinia earned on insurance; recent guides list it as excluded or capped)
- Education fees paid via third-party apps (verify — direct/college-portal education spends may still earn)

## Reward Caps
- Overall SmartBuy accelerated cap: 15,000 bonus Reward Points per calendar month, shared across all SmartBuy categories.
- SmartBuy brand-voucher sub-cap (effective 1 July 2026): accelerated points on Gyftr/Woohoo vouchers capped at 3,000 bonus RP per month. Base 5 RP/₹150 continues beyond this. (verify)
- Redemption caps: up to 2 lakh RP per statement cycle; redemption for flights, hotels and airmiles capped at ~1.5 lakh RP per month; Apple/Tanishq redemptions limited to 70% of bill value. (verify)

## Milestone Benefits
- Welcome / renewal benefit: 12,500 Reward Points on fee realisation and card activation (and again each year on renewal-fee payment). (verify exact figure)
- Complimentary Club Marriott membership (first year; renewable) — up to 20% off F&B, stays and spa at participating Marriott properties in Asia-Pacific, plus room-upgrade certificates in the first year only.
- Lounge access: unlimited complimentary domestic and international lounge access via Priority Pass (primary + add-on).
- No fixed rupee spend milestone vouchers are standard on Infinia beyond the above (verify).

## Redemption Options
- SmartBuy flights & hotels: 1 RP = ₹1 (1:1 conversion of points to booking value).
- Apple products / Tanishq vouchers: 1 RP = ₹1, but max 70% of bill payable via points.
- AirMiles / partner transfer: convert to airline & hotel partners (see below).
- Products & catalogue vouchers: 1 RP = ₹0.50.
- Cashback / statement credit: 1 RP = ₹0.30.

## Point Valuation
- Assumed ₹1.00 per Reward Point for SmartBuy flights/hotels and Apple/Tanishq redemptions.
- ₹0.50 per point for catalogue products/vouchers.
- ₹0.30 per point for cashback.
- Airline transfers can exceed ₹1/point in premium-cabin sweet spots (e.g., KrisFlyer, Flying Blue), but only at favourable 1:1 partners. (verify)

## Annual Fee & Waiver
- Annual / renewal fee: ₹12,500 + GST for the current Metal Edition (the older non-metal Infinia carried ₹10,000 + GST). (verify)
- Renewal fee waiver: spend ₹10 lakh or more in the preceding 12 months to get the next year's renewal fee waived.
