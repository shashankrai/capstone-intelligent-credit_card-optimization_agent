# SBI Cashback Credit Card — Terms & Reward Rules

Issuer: SBI Card
Reward currency: Direct Cashback (statement credit)
Retrieved date: 2026-07-15
Source: compiled from public sources listed below — NOT official issuer documentation; verify current terms with the issuer.

## Reward Earning Structure
- Online spends: 5% cashback on eligible online purchases, with no merchant restriction (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, Swiggy, etc. all qualify).
- Offline spends (POS at merchant outlets): 1% cashback on eligible in-store purchases.
- Flights/hotels booked online: Generally qualify for the 5% online rate, provided the merchant reports a travel-related MCC (e.g., MCC 4722). Bookings reported under the railway MCC 4112 (a known issue on some OTAs such as Cleartrip and EaseMyTrip) earn no cashback. Testing with a small transaction first is advised. (verify — behavior depends on the MCC the specific merchant transmits)

## Exclusions (no cashback earned)
No cashback is earned on the following categories:
- Rent / rental payments
- Wallet loads
- Fuel
- Utility payments — fully excluded (no threshold; the card earns 0% on utilities) (verify)
- Insurance
- School & educational services
- Jewellery
- Railways (MCC 4112)
- EMI transactions (Merchant EMI / Flexipay EMI)
- Quasi-cash and member financial institution transactions
- Card/gift/novelty/souvenir shops
- New exclusions effective April 1, 2026: Digital gaming (MCC 7993, 7994, 5816), Tolls (MCC 4784), Government-related transactions (MCC 9222, 9311, 9402)

Note: unlike some other SBI reward cards, this card does not use an "above ₹X utilities earn nothing" threshold — utilities are wholly excluded from cashback.

## Reward Caps
Effective April 1, 2026 (revised structure):
- Online cashback: capped at ₹2,000 per statement cycle (≈ ₹40,000 of online spend at 5%).
- Offline cashback: capped at ₹2,000 per statement cycle.
- Total cashback: maximum ₹4,000 per statement cycle (reduced from the earlier ₹5,000 combined limit).

## Milestone Benefits
None. This is a flat-cashback card with no spend-milestone rewards, vouchers, or bonus tiers.

## Point Valuation
Not applicable — cashback is credited directly as a statement credit (₹1 = ₹1). There are no reward points to value or convert.

## Transfer Partners
None — cashback cannot be transferred. It is auto-credited to the card statement (within ~2 working days of statement generation) and cannot be moved to airline/hotel or other loyalty programs.

## Annual Fee & Waiver
- Joining fee: ₹999 + GST (charged in the first month's statement).
- Annual/renewal fee: ₹999 + GST (from the 2nd year).
- Waiver: annual fee waived if total spend of ₹2,00,000 or more is made in the preceding membership year.
