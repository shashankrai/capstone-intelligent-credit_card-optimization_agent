# Axis Atlas Credit Card — Terms, Reward Rules & Transfer Partners

Issuer: Axis Bank
Reward currency: EDGE Miles
Retrieved date: 2026-07-15
Source: compiled from public sources listed below — NOT official issuer documentation; verify current terms with the issuer.

## Reward Earning Structure
- Flights / airlines (direct): 5 EDGE Miles per ₹100 (accelerated) up to the monthly travel cap; 2 EDGE Miles per ₹100 above the cap.
- Hotels (direct): 5 EDGE Miles per ₹100 (accelerated) up to the monthly travel cap; 2 EDGE Miles per ₹100 above the cap.
- Travel-direct via Axis Travel EDGE portal: 5 EDGE Miles per ₹100 (accelerated), same monthly cap.
- General / all other eligible spends: 2 EDGE Miles per ₹100 (base rate).
- Note: The accelerated 5x rate applies only to direct airline/hotel merchants and the Travel EDGE portal — bookings via third-party online travel agencies (e.g., MakeMyTrip, Cleartrip) earn only the base 2x. (verify)

## Exclusions (no EDGE Miles earned)
- Rent
- Wallet loads
- Fuel
- Government institutions / services
- Insurance
- Utilities
- Gold / jewellery
- (These spends are also excluded from tier-qualification calculations.) Note: education is not consistently listed as an exclusion for Atlas across sources. (verify)

## Reward Caps
- Accelerated (5x) travel earning is capped at ₹2,00,000 of cumulative travel spend (airline + hotel + Travel EDGE) per calendar month; travel spend beyond that in the month earns only the base 2 EDGE Miles per ₹100.
- No separate cap on base-rate (2x) earning is documented. (verify)

## Milestone Benefits
- Welcome bonus: 2,500 EDGE Miles on completing the first transaction within 37 days of card issuance.
- ₹3,00,000 annual (anniversary-year) spend milestone: 2,500 EDGE Miles.
- ₹7,50,000 spend: additional 2,500 EDGE Miles (also upgrades to Gold tier).
- ₹15,00,000 spend: additional 5,000 EDGE Miles (also upgrades to Platinum tier).
- Total milestone bonus potential: up to 10,000 EDGE Miles per year (2,500 + 2,500 + 5,000).
- Tier renewal benefit (separate from milestones, credited on annual-fee payment): Gold tier = 2,500 EDGE Miles; Platinum tier = 5,000 EDGE Miles. Silver tier = none.
- Tier structure: Silver (default), Gold (≥₹7.5L eligible spend), Platinum (≥₹15L eligible spend); tiers also drive lounge access (Silver 8 domestic + 4 international; Gold 12 + 6; Platinum 18 + 12).

## Redemption Options
- Transfer to airline/hotel loyalty partners via the Travel EDGE portal ("Points/Miles Transfer") — the primary high-value route.
- Redeem on the Axis Travel EDGE platform for flights/hotels directly at a 1:1 value (1 EDGE Mile = ₹1).
- Transfer fee: ₹199 + GST (approx. ₹235) per transfer to a partner. (verify)
- Access via the Axis Mobile app or the Travel EDGE website.

## Point Valuation
- Base/floor value: 1 EDGE Mile ≈ ₹1 (redemption on Travel EDGE at 1:1).
- Effective value via transfer: up to ≈ ₹2 per EDGE Mile when transferred at the standard 1:2 ratio to airline partners (depending on partner award-chart value); materially lower (~₹0.5 equivalent) for the new 2:1 partners.

## Annual Fee & Waiver
- Joining fee: ₹5,000 + GST.
- Annual/renewal fee: ₹5,000 + GST (second year onwards).
- Waiver: The Atlas card does not offer a spend-based annual-fee waiver; instead the value is returned via tier renewal bonus miles (2,500 Gold / 5,000 Platinum) credited on fee payment. Silver-tier holders receive no renewal miles. (verify)
- Note: As of mid-2026, Axis Bank has paused new applications for the Atlas card; existing cardholders retain all benefits.
